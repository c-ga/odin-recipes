Pizza

Description

A dish of Italian origin consisting of a usually round, flat base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and often various other ingredients, which is then baked at a high temperature, traditionally in a wood-fired oven.

Ingredients

  • Artisan Pizza Dough
  • All-purpose flour, for dusting
  • Semolina flour, for dusting
  • 1/4cup Basic Pizza Sauce
  • 3 ounces of shredded low-moisture mozzarella
  • Fresh basil or oregano leaves, for garnishing

Directions

  1. Let chilled covered dough stand at room temperature until dough is cool. 1-2 hours
  2. Preheat to 500*F for 30 minutes with a baking steel pan on middle rack
  3. Form a C-shape with the outer edge of your hand and press firmly inside dough border
  4. Stretch and shape the dough until satisfied, spread dough with Pizza Sauce
  5. Top with shredded mozzarella
  6. Gently shake pizza peel with prepared pie to loosen, unload pizza onto preheated pan in the oven
  7. Bake until edges of crust have puffed slightly, and rotate until it is cooked through and the crust is charred and risen in spots
  8. Remove from oven and garnish with fresh basil or oregano, slice into wedges if desired
