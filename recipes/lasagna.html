Lasagna

Description

One of the most beloved foods in the world, this is a traditional Italian Lasagna made with Bolognese ragu and cheese sauce.

Ingredients

  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Carrot
  • Celery
  • Beef
  • Canned tomato and tomato paste
  • Red wine
  • Seasonings - stock cubes, bay leaves, etc
  • Butter, flour, milk, cheese
  • Lasagna sheets

Directions

  1. Smear - a bit of meat sauce on the base first
  2. Layer 1 - Top with meat sauce, bit of white sauce
  3. Layer 2 - Lay out more lasagna sheets, then top with more meat sauce and more white sauce
  4. Layer 3 - again, lasagna sheets, meat sauce then white sauce
  5. Topping - Cover with lasagna sheets, pour remaining white sauce then sprinkle with cheese
  6. Bake - until fully cooked and edges are browning
