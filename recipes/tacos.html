Tacos

Description

A taco is a traditional Mexican food consisting of a small hand-sized corn or wheat based tortilla topped with a filling. The tortilla is then folded around the filling and eaten by hand.

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • Brown onion
  • Cumin
  • Coriander
  • Paprika
  • Cayenne pepper
  • Regular beef mince
  • Crushed tomatoes
  • Jumbo taco shells
  • Iceberg lettuce leaves, shredded
  • Tomatoes, chopped
  • Cheese

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 180*C
  2. Heat oil in large pan
  3. Add onion, cook and stir until softened
  4. Add cumin, coriander, paprika and cayenne pepper
  5. Add mince, cook for 8 minutes or until browned
  6. Add crushed tomato, cook and stir for 5 minutes
  7. Meanwhile, heat taco shells
  8. Divide beef mixture and salad tacos evenly between shells
  9. Top with lettuce, chopped tomato and cheese
  10. Serve
